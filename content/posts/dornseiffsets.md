# What are Dornseiff sets?

Dornseiff Sets are words sets groupped by semantic criteria. According to the 8th edition of the dictionary Dornseiff (_Der deutsche Wortschatz nach Sachgruppen_), there are currently 22 main groups (which I took the liberty of denominating them as [``main Dornseiff sets``](/maindornseiffs/)) and 970 domain groups (here denominated as [``domain Dornseiff sets``](/domaindornseiffs/)). 

The 8th edition of the dictionary (published in 2004) was created using data of the [**Leipzig Corpora Collection**](https://corpora.uni-leipzig.de/en?corpusId=deu_typical-mixed_2018), one of three the main sources used in this site.

For more information about Dornseiff sets, check [here](https://wortschatz.uni-leipzig.de/en/documentation/faq) and [here](https://www.degruyter.com/view/product/61548).
